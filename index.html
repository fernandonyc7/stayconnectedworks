Everything you need

Powerful tools, simple to use

Everything a service business owner needs, without the complexity or the bloated price tag.

📍
Live GPS Tracking
See exactly where your whole team is in real time. Know when they arrive and leave each job automatically.
  • Real-time map of all employees
  • Auto check-in at job location
  • Time-on-site history & logs
  • Mileage tracking for payroll
📅
Drag & Drop Scheduling
Visual calendar that makes scheduling effortless. Assign jobs, avoid conflicts, and send automatic reminders to clients and staff.
💳
Payments & Invoicing
Send invoices instantly after each job, accept credit cards, and track exactly who has paid — all without leaving the app.
👥
Client & Employee Management
Full profiles for clients (job history, special notes, addresses, preferences) and employees (hours worked, performance, assigned jobs). Everything organized and searchable in one place. No more sticky notes or lost texts.
📊
Reports & Analytics
Revenue trends, job completion rates, and top-performing employees — clear dashboards you can actually use to grow your business.
📱
Employee Mobile App
Your staff get a simple app to see their daily schedule, navigate to jobs, and mark tasks complete as they go.
⭐
Automated Reviews
After every completed job, clients get an automatic review request. Build your reputation on autopilot.
🔔
Smart Notifications
Reminders, alerts, and updates go to the right people at the right time — clients, employees, and you as the owner.
💬
Two-Way Texting from YOUR Phone Number  Competitor Advantage
Unlike other platforms that text your clients from a generic company number, Stay Connected Works lets you text directly from your own business phone number. Clients see your number, can save it, and text you back — all managed inside the app. Two-way conversation history is saved per client automatically.
📲
Auto-Text Triggers
Automatically send texts when jobs are confirmed, when staff are on their way, when a job is completed, or when an invoice is ready — all from your number, hands-free.
Why this matters

Other platforms text your clients from their number. We use yours.

MaidPad, Jobber, and most competitors send texts from a platform number your clients don't recognize. With Stay Connected Works, every text comes from your real business number — building trust, familiarity, and a direct relationship with every client.
